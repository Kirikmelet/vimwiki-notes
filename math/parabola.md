*Find Y from Vertex*

3. Replace value of X with Vertex's
4. Simplify Formula

*Convert Standard to Vertex Form*

_Example Equation_

if Y = X^2^ + 6X + - 5

1. Find Half of B
Y = X^2^ + 6X + 3^2^ - 5 - 3^2^

2. Square it
Y = X(X + 3) - 5 - 3^2^ 

3. Simplify it 
Y = X(X + 3) - 5 - 9

Y = X(X + 3) - 14
